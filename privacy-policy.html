Privacy Policy for ChatOffline

Effective date: 30 June 2024

Introduction

Welcome to ChatOffline. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal data when you use our mobile application and website.

Data Collection

ChatOffline collects the following types of information:

  • Username: Used to identify you within the app and stored using iCloud.
  • Device Information: Basic information about your device for the purpose of connecting with nearby devices (e.g., device name).

Data Usage

We use the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service.
  • To notify you about changes to our service.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To monitor the usage of our service.

Data Sharing

We may share your personal data with third parties in the following situations:

  • With your consent.
  • For legal reasons.
  • To protect and defend our rights and property.
  • To prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with the service.

User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • The right to access and update your data.
  • The right to request the deletion of your data.
  • The right to withdraw consent.
  • The right to data portability.

Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Use of Technologies

ChatOffline uses various technologies to ensure seamless communication and data transfer:

  • Multipeer Connectivity: To connect and communicate with nearby devices.
  • Core Bluetooth: To facilitate device discovery and communication.
  • CloudKit: To store usernames in the cloud.
  • Push Notifications: To notify users of new messages and updates.

Security of Messages

Please note that messages exchanged through ChatOffline are transmitted locally between devices and are not stored on any server. Because anyone with the app who is nearby can join the chat, messages can be seen by those who connect. Therefore, messages should not be considered secure. Users should not share sensitive information through ChatOffline.
